Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How quickly can you respond to inquiries? +

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business hours (Sunday–Thursday, 9 AM – 6 PM GST). For urgent matters, contact us via WhatsApp for immediate assistance.

Do you offer bulk discounts? +

Yes, we provide competitive pricing for bulk orders and corporate clients. Please contact our sales team with your requirements for a customized quotation.

What is your payment and delivery policy? +

We accept various payment methods including bank transfer, cash, and credit terms for eligible businesses. Delivery is arranged based on location and can typically be completed within 1-3 business days in the UAE.

Can you provide customized solutions? +

Absolutely! We specialize in custom software development, dashboard solutions, and tailored IT services. Contact our solutions team to discuss your specific requirements.

What warranty and support do you provide? +

All products come with manufacturer warranties. We provide technical support, after-sales service, and can arrange extended warranty packages as needed.

Are you available for on-site consultations? +

Yes, our team can arrange on-site consultations for corporate clients, system integrators, and large projects. Contact us to schedule a visit.
